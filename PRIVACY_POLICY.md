# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

## Overview

Open on Map for Google Search is a browser extension that adds an **Open on map** button to Google Search result pages when a location is shown.

This extension is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly.

## Data Collection

The extension does **not** collect personal data.

The extension does **not** collect, store, sell, share, or transmit:

- your name
- your email address
- your browsing history
- your search history
- your location history
- your IP address
- page contents for analytics
- cookies
- account information
- payment information

## How The Extension Works

The extension runs locally in your browser on Google Search result pages.

It looks at the visible Google Search page to detect whether Google is already showing a place, address, business, map card, or location panel.

If a location is detected, the extension adds an **Open on map** button to the page.

When you click that button, your browser opens Google Maps in a new tab using either:

- the place name shown on the Google Search page, or
- coordinates already present in Google map links or map previews on the page.

## Data Sent To The Developer

No data is sent to the developer.

The extension has no developer server, no analytics system, no tracking pixel, and no remote logging.

## Data Sent To Third Parties

When you click **Open on map**, your browser opens Google Maps.

That means Google Maps receives the place name or coordinates needed to show the map result. This is the same type of request that happens when you manually search for a place in Google Maps.

This extension does not send that information to any other third party.

## Permissions

The extension requests access to Google Search pages so it can add the **Open on map** button when a location result is visible.

The extension does not use these permissions to collect user data.

## Remote Code

The extension does not load remote code.

All extension code is included in the extension package and can be inspected in the open source repository.

## Advertising And Tracking

The extension does not contain ads.

The extension does not use tracking, analytics, affiliate links, or behavioral profiling.

## Children

The extension is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes To This Policy

If the extension changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated before or at the same time as the extension update.
